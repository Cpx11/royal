Royal Clothing Team

Serving Irrua & surroundings with premium tailoring

Royal Clothing Fashion Home is Irrua's premier destination for bespoke African fashion. Located on Momodu Road, Maryland Eguare, we've built a reputation for excellence in crafting ceremonial and everyday wear that blends heritage with modern elegance.

Whether you need a magnificent Agbada for your wedding, a sharp Senator wear for a corporate event, or a custom Kaftan for a celebration, our skilled artisans bring your vision to life with exceptional fabric selection and flawless execution.

We work with clients across Irrua, Benin City, and throughout Edo State — offering both in-person appointments and online consultations for those who cannot visit our atelier.

Measurements Guide

Submit Your Measurements

Can't visit us in person? No problem. Fill out your measurements below and we'll create your garment from anywhere in Nigeria. Use our guide to measure accurately at home.

📏 Measurement Guide

💡

Stand straight and breathe normally when measuring. Have someone help for more accuracy.

📐

Use a flexible measuring tape. Add 2–3 inches to chest and waist for comfort allowance.

📸

You can also send a full-body photo and we'll estimate measurements.
